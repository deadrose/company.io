About Us


Bingham Theater Camp began in the summer of 2012, with 24 children eager to learn about how to dance, to sing, and, of course, to perform on stage. Our very first performance together was Hello, Dolly!, and it was a huge success! And so, for the next 6 years, we would keep producing plays with new and old faces.

The People Involved

Kaitlyn Bingham



As the one of the main founders of Bingham Theater Camp, Katie is in charge of just about everything, but she is the most involved in the improv and acting sessions. She has had an interest in theater since fith grade, where she performed in her first official play, Beauty and the Beast Jr. For years she would do all she can to get better and singing, dancing, and acting. By the time she had graduated, she knew that she wanted her career to be helping children learn how to perform a musical and have fun whle doing it. She's been keeping up that dream for years, and it looks like there's no stopping her!

Much like Katie, Marcia also had an interest in acting at a young age, only she has been acting since she was five! She does just about everything Katie does, as she is the other co-founder. If it weren't for her, there wouldn't be as much fun memories as there are now!

Marcia Rayner



Klaudia Welch



Klaudia joined us in our first year as our choreographer. She takes care of teaching everyone dance moves for the show, warmup stretches, and other exercises. She started her dancing career when she was fifteen and started taking ballet lessons. Since then she has moved on to more complicated dance performances, but always makes time in the summer to help teach our kids how to dance!

Gail is in charge with teaching the kids how to sing. Her responsibilities include vocal warmups, teaching the actual song, and much more. She has been with us since day one, and we can't imagine a world without her! There is no denying that she maybe the best singer in the world, along with best teacher.
